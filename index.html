What is Symptomis

Symptomis is an advanced virtual symptom detector that diagnose and medicates on the basis of symptoms entered by users. It also provides the discriptions and uses of medicines accordingly.

  • Most doctors agree that online symptom checkers are can encourage people with life-threatening symptoms to seek urgent attention, potentially saving lives. They’re also useful for reassuring patients who may have sought urgent care when they didn’t need to.
  • According to who most treatments becomes unsuccessful just because of delay in treatments. So it is important that if a person is not able to visit doctors then also he/she should not ignore the symptoms.
  • By the help of nlp and ai based symptom detector, symptomis would be a big success in the field of techno-medical science.
  • With its mobile app it would be available almost everywhere on technology so that people could detect their deseases earlier by not avoiding their the symptoms.

So these are the features and future planes of our web/app symptomis. "Treatment without prevention is simply unsustainable,and treatment with big delay is useless... "
